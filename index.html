Our Mission @ FoodStop.com

Foodstop is a fictitious restaurant search and discovery service founded in 2017 by Harold Okafor, Digvijay Dond and Sudhir Hebbalkar who are Students of University of Central Missouri. It currently operates in Kansas City, Overland Park and Warrensburg city limits. The service allows users to select restaurants and/or their preferred dishes. Our mission is to bring people and great places to eat closer together. This website will help you filter Restaurants according to the cost, choice and convenience - Our 3 C's.

To enjoy our full suite of offerings, you will be required to sign up if you are new or login, if you are an already registered member.

Hope you enjoy our services and please drop a feedback.
